Time, Space, and Complexity

Time complexity is a conceptual framework used to quantify the efficiency of algorithms in terms of the time they require to complete as a function of the input size. It's a critical metric for evaluating how scalable and efficient an algorithm is under various conditions.

The concept of Space Complexity is pivotal in assessing the amount of memory an algorithm needs to execute successfully. Particularly for large datasets, maintaining a low Space Complexity, often symbolized as O(n), is essential for optimizing resource consumption and ensuring efficiency.
